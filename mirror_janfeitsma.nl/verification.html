Verification status

The algorithms were checked and even improved a bit by Warren D Smith. A public discussion thread can be found on Yahoo primenumbers group.
The numerical verification is still in progress.
  • category S: not yet finished (David Cleaver and Jeff Gilchrist)
  • category E: 90% finished (David Cleaver and Jeff Gilchrist)
  • Wieferich: verified OK (Warren D Smith)

Finally, result counts were checked against any available data. In particular, Richard Pinch shared all 4279356 Carmichael numbers below 264, which were found to be identical to those produced here.
